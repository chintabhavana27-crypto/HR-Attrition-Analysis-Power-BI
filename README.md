# HR Attrition & Employee Insights Dashboard

## Overview
This repository contains a Power BI dashboard designed to analyze and visualize employee attrition data. The dashboard provides in-depth insights into various factors affecting employee retention such as department, age, income, gender, work-life balance and job satisfaction. The analysis offers HR teams actionable recommendations to improve retention strategies and reduce turnover rates.

## Key Features
- *Attrition by Department*: Breakdown of attrition rates across HR, R&D and Sales departments.
- *Attrition by Age Band*: Insight into how attrition varies across different age groups.
- *Attrition by Income*: Analyzes the correlation between income levels and attrition rates.
- *Attrition by Gender*: Gender-based comparison of employee attrition rates.
- *Work-Life Balance*: Shows the relationship between work-life balance scores and attrition rates.
- *Job Satisfaction & Attrition*: Examines the effect of job satisfaction levels on employee attrition.

## HR Insights & Recommendations

### Strategy Insights:
- *Overall Attrition Rate*: The attrition rate stands at 16.12% indicating a need for HR interventions.
- *Departments with High Attrition*: Sales and R&D departments have the highest attrition rates.
- *Age-Related Trends*: Employees aged 18-35 are more likely to leave the company.
- *Income and Attrition*: Employees in lower income brackets show higher attrition rates.
- *Overtime and Attrition*: Overtime is positively correlated with employee attrition.
- *Gender Disparity*: Male employees show a higher attrition rate compared to female employees.
- *Job Role Risk*: Certain roles such as Sales Representatives face higher attrition risk.

### HR Recommendations:
- *Retention Focus*: Prioritize retention efforts in Sales and R&D departments.
- *Improve Compensation*: Increase salary and benefits for employees in lower income bands.
- *Reduce Overtime*: Minimize overtime hours to reduce attrition.
- *Career Growth*: Support early-career employees with growth opportunities and clear career paths.
- *Work-Life Balance*: Strengthen work-life balance policies to retain employees.
- *Monitor High-Risk Employees*: Proactively identify and monitor employees at high attrition risk.
